About Zecheng Electronics

Foshan Zecheng Electronics Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the production of NTC negative temperature coefficient thermistors, NTC temperature sensors, and other sensitive components. We are one of the largest professional manufacturers of sensitive components in China, with our own NTC thermistor and temperature sensor production facilities and comprehensive product production and aging stabilization solutions. We are a company dedicated to providing negative temperature coefficient measurement products and can provide customers with special specifications.

Since its establishment, the company has maintained a good development momentum, with sales increasing rapidly every year. Our products are widely used in industrial electronic equipment, communications, power, transportation, medical equipment, household appliances, test instruments, power supply equipment, and other fields.

With a spirit of integrity and cooperation, we professionally provide customers with excellent products and comprehensive temperature measurement technical support, reflecting the value of products and achieving a win-win service policy.
